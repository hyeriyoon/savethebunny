About this game

Guess the secret number and save the bunny from the cage. You have 10 chances and some clues are here.
Enter numbers without duplicates.
The number ranges from 0 to 9.

If you match both number and position, the number of 'strike(s)' will increase by that number.
If you match number only, the number of 'ball(s)' will increase by that number.

When you match all the numbers and positions, bunny will appear. Let's play and save your bunny!
